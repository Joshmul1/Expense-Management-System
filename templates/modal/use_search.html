How to use the search

By typing what you are looking for in the search box, it will automatically filter what is typed.
If you are looking to filter by more than one search term, whether it be in different categories you can do this by simply separating each term with a space.
For example:
"Entertainment Food 26-01-2020"

This will filter to all expenses with "Entertainment", "Food", and with a date of "26-01-2020" as its details Each column has its own sorting button. Once clicked it will organise and sort that column accordingly.
